Noise label classification

Machine Learning

Label noise will have a huge impact on the classification results. In this project, two noise robustness classifiers (ResNEt and LeNet neural networks) were tested in two datasets: FashionMINIST0.5, FashionMINIST0.6.

T-Revision method is used to estimate the transition matrix without knowing the anchor point. With the transition matrix, the accuracy of the classifiers have been improved.
